<p><p>Supervisory circuits are essential components in electronic systems, offering functions like voltage monitoring and system reset. The market for these circuits can be categorized mainly into SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD types are compact and suited for high-density applications, making them popular in modern electronics. Conversely, Through Hole types are known for their robust connections and are often used in applications requiring higher power handling. Both types serve distinct needs based on design preferences and application requirements.</p></p>
